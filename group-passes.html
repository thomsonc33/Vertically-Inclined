GROUP PASSES

Looking for something new and exciting to do for your next event? Bring your friends, family or co-workers to Vertically Inclined. We provide group bookings for birthday parties, school programs, team building or group climbing just for fun!

BIRTHDAY PARTIES

Vertically Inclined Birthdays are geared for fun and packed with climbing excitement! We include all the necessary rental equipment and excellent instructors that will guide you through your celebration. Birthday sessions include 2 hours with the instructors, and 30 additional minutes for food, gifts, cake, etc. afterwards.

SCHEDULE

Birthday Parties must be pre-booked and can be scheduled Fridays at 6pm & 8pm or Saturdays and Sundays starting at 12pm, 2pm, 4pm & 6pm.

CANCELLATIONS

Birthday Party bookings cancellations done more than 48 hours ahead of the session will be refunded.

AGES

Birthday Parties can be booked for groups aged 5 years and older. The maximum is 10 climbers ages 5-9 years and for ages 10 and up the maximum is 20 climbers.

TEAM BUILDING

Whether success is measured in vertical feet or in dollars, team functioning is the critical factor. Use the tools of climbing and action-based learning to take your team to new heights! With 20 years of experience, Vertically Inclined is a leader in providing unique action-based learning programs.

SCHOOL PROGRAMS

The school Just Climbing session is our most popular program. At Vertically Inclined we provide an opportunity for all students to experience the benefits of climbing in a supportive atmosphere. From kindergarten to college…we can provide a session that’s right for your group!

GROUP CLIMBING

Whether you’re looking to celebrate your sports team’s winning season, or to rejoice the fact that someone is getting married, the Just Climbing Session is ideal for your crew. This 2-hour exposure session will get you and your group climbing in no time flat.
