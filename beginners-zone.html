FIRST TIME?

Rock climbing may seem daunting at first – but with some basic information, you can climb with confidence. Our experienced instructors are here to guide you every step of the way.

BOOK A TIME

The Climbing Adventure is an introductory program that includes instruction on the use of the basic rope system preparing you for climbing fun! Also included are a facility orientation and all the equipment that you’ll need. Take a look at our calendar to plan a visit.

CLIMBING TYPES

BOULDERING

AUTO-BELAY

Auto belays are devices that allow individual climbers to climb the wall and come back to the ground on their own. The tethers on these devices retract as the climber moves up the wall and then, when the climber lets go of the wall, the device lowers them to the ground at a consistent speed. Climbers can use them to climb to the top of the walls without needing a partner controlling the ropes. And the ride back down is pretty darn fun too!

NOTE: young children will require assistance from an adult or older child in attaching to the auto belays.

Is it safe?

Indoor climbing has removed the major dangers from the sport. However, like any activity indoor climbing has associated risks. We technically cannot say that climbing is a safe sport (or for that matter that any sport is safe) because humans are unpredictable and can do completely unexpected things. We make every effort to account for all possible situations through continuously upgrading our programs and training our staff.
