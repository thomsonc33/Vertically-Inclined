AUTO-BELAY ADVENTURE

This course introduces auto-belay climbing. Auto-belays are devices that allow individual climbers to climb the wall and come back to the ground on their own – no need for a partner.

DETAILS

BOULDERING

This course explains the fundamentals of Bouldering, which involves climbing lower to the ground without using the ropes and therefore, the only equipment required is climbing shoes and chalk.
